Tongatapu, Tonga

My island, Tongatapu, is known for its friendly people, strong cultural traditions, and beautiful natural scenery. It is the main island of the Kingdom of Tonga and home to many historical landmarks such as Ha'amonga 'a Maui, an ancient trilithon. Tongatapu is a place where culture, history, and hospitality come together, making it a truly special island in the South Pacific.
