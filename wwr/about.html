Dry Oar Rafting Company

Welcome to Dry Oar, where our passion for the river runs as deep as the canyons we explore!

Our mission is to provide unforgettable, safe, and exhilarating whitewater experiences for adventurers of all skill levels. We believe life's best stories are written on the water.

History

Dry Oar was founded in 1985 by two river guides, Alex and Jordan, who dreamt of sharing the raw beauty of the canyonlands with the world. Starting with just two borrowed rafts, the company quickly grew based on our reputation for unmatched safety and legendary camp meals. Today, we run trips across three major rivers, but our core belief remains the same: treat the river with respect and the clients like family.
