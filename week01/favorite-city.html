Shanghai, China

Shanghai is a global metropolis and financial hub, often called the "Pearl of the Orient." It is renowned for its magnificent skyline along the Huangpu River, especially the futuristic towers in the Lujiazui area of Pudong.

The city beautifully preserves its history, from the colonial architecture of **The Bund** to the traditional gardens and alleyways of the Old City. The contrast between these historic sites and the modern skyscrapers makes Shanghai truly unique.
